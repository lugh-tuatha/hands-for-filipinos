HELP FILIPINOS & SAVE LIVES

Save the Children works hard to help children gain access to quality education and health services, support them in times of great need, and protect them from risks and harm. We plan and budgets to ensure that children, especially those living in poor communities, have access to quality basic services and have their rights protected.

Our generous supporters in the Philippines and throughout the world help make our work possible.

As part of the Hands for the filipinos network, we share a global vision and strategy aimed at creating better lives for filipinos in the Philippines. We protect and support filipino in need. We save lives in emergencies. We speak up for children's rights.
